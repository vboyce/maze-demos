Maze demos

The following are some demos of the Maze task, using automated distractor generation and jsPsych for the web interface. Note that data from these demos is not being saved.

Demos

  • Targeted sentences in redo mode with results display Items from Maze Made Easy
  • Target and filler sentences (no redo) Items and distractors are subset from Maze Made Easy
  • Short vignettes Items from Altmann and Steedman (1988)
  • Natural Stories story One of the passages from the Natural Stories Corpus, with distractors from A-Maze of Natural Stories
